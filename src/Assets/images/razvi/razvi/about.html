Arsalan Raza Khan

Arsalan Raza Khan Azhari (Urdu: ارسلان رضا خان ازہری; born 26 July, 1994 ) also known as..... , is an Indian, Barelvi Muslim, cleric and mufti, Islamic scholar, theologian, spiritual leader and he is the great-grandson of Ahmed Raza Khan Barelvi who was considered to be a Mujaddid by his followers and was the eponymous founder of the Barelvi movement.

Early life and education:

He went to school at Dar-ul-Uloom Faiz-ur-Rasool, Baraon Shareef, Madrasa Manzar-e-Islam of the Dargah Aala Hazrat. He graduated from the Al-Azhar University in Egypt in the Arabic literature and Islamic Studies with specialisation in Hadith and Tafseer from 2016-2020, He studied all Islamic sciences in depth with his honourable teachers. He grasp on Fiqh, Hadith and History is well known.

Spiritual life:

He is a spiritual follower and successor of his maternal grandfather Mustafa Raza Khan Qadri, the Grand Mufti of Razvi Darul Ifta in the Qadriyya-Barkatiyya-Razviyya-Nooriya order of Sufism

Literary work:

  • Fatwa Razviya Darul ifta.
  • Tarbiyat e lauho wa Kalam
  • Risaltan Raiyatan
  • Al Wardata Sharhul farda

Academic excellence:

He secured 1 postion and topper of each class at Darul Uloom Faizur Rasool Baraon Shareef

Excellence award:

On the occasion of Urse Huzur Shuaib ul Awliya He honoured with academic excellence award by the hand of Honorable Gulam Abdul Qadir Alvi (Mufakkir-e-slam) at Darul Uloom Faizur Rasool Baraon Shareef
