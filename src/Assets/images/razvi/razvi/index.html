Razvi Darul Ifta

The "Razvi Darul Ifta Bareilly" fatwa website offers authentic Hanafi Ahlus Sunnah guidance. It provides a wide range of Islamic resources, including books, articles, and detailed fatwas on various topics. Users can align their lives with Islamic teachings and contribute to Islamic education and welfare through the trust donation section. This platform upholds traditional Islamic knowledge while addressing modern-day inquiries.
